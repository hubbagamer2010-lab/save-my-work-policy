Privacy Policy for
Save My Work Pro

Save My Work Pro respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your data. This document explains what data we handle and how we handle it — transparently and securely.

No Data Collection
100% Local Storage
No Third Parties
No Tracking
01

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. No personal data such as your name, email address, or credentials is ever collected, processed, or sent anywhere.

Zero personal data leaves your device — ever. We have no servers, no databases, and no interest in your information.
02

Local Storage Usage

All user-entered text is stored locally on your device using the browser's built-in storage system (such as chrome.storage.local). This data never leaves your browser and is used solely to restore your input in case of accidental data loss — such as page refresh, browser crashes, or accidental navigation.

Think of it like a local notepad that only exists on your machine. No cloud, no sync, no external access.
03

No Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, lease, rent, or transfer any user data to third parties — period. There are no partnerships, advertising networks, or analytics providers involved with this extension. Your data stays yours, always.

04

No Tracking

This extension does not track your browsing history, user activity, keystrokes, or behavioral patterns. It operates strictly within the boundaries of its core functionality — saving and restoring your work. No analytics scripts, no telemetry, no fingerprinting.

05

No Remote Code or Servers

All functionality runs entirely within the extension on your local machine. No external servers, APIs, CDNs, or remote code execution is involved. The extension operates in a fully offline-capable manner — no internet connection is required for it to function.

Everything runs client-side. There are no network requests, no remote dependencies, and no external scripts.
06

User Control

You have full and complete control over your data at all times. Any stored data can be cleared instantly by:

  • Clearing the extension's storage from browser settings
  • Using the extension's built-in clear/reset option
  • Uninstalling/removing the extension entirely
